ConsultAI OS is the consulting plane of the playbook: situation-first, stage-aware, and built to turn ambiguous AI problems into evidence-backed decisions.

{/* truncate */}

## The shape of the journey

Users move through a lifecycle (legacy stages 0–19), with soft gates, registers, and exportable deliverables. Product direction maps that detail under an **8D** journey (Define → Deliver) while keeping stage tools available when needed.

## Where to click first

1. Open **[ConsultAI OS](https://sudip-ai-playbook.github.io/consult)** from the Hub.
2. Create or select a **workspace** (client / engagement).
3. Pick a **business situation**, then filter frameworks by lifecycle step.
4. Run a **workshop** card: agenda, voting, decisions, and actions.
5. Export an HTML or Excel pack when you need a stakeholder artefact.
