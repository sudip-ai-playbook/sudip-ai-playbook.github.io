Practise sponsor and service-line engagement with [Leadership Business Engagement](/roadmaps/leadership-engage-business-leaders) and [Leadership: How to Engage with Business and Service-Line Leaders](/articles/leadership-engage-business-service-line-leaders). Remove executive obstacles with [Remove Delivery Blockers](/roadmaps/leadership-remove-delivery-blockers) and [Leadership: Remove Delivery Blockers](/articles/leadership-remove-delivery-blockers). For trusted innovation decisions, use [Risk, Governance and Assurance](/roadmaps/leadership-risk-governance-assurance) and [Leadership: Make Risk, Governance and Assurance Decisions](/articles/leadership-risk-governance-assurance-decisions). Protect professional standards with [Quality, Independence and Trust](/roadmaps/leadership-quality-independence-trust) and [Leadership: Protect Quality, Independence and Trust](/articles/leadership-protect-quality-independence-and-trust). Treat the firm as a transformation client with [Organisation-Wide Transformation](/roadmaps/leadership-organisation-wide-transformation) and [Leadership: Lead Organisation-Wide Transformation](/articles/leadership-organisation-wide-transformation).
